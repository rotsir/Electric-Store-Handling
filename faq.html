Knowledge Base (FAQ)

Common queries indexed for rapid retrieval.

01. What is your emergency response time? ▼

For critical infrastructure failures and immediate safety hazards, our dispatch units are active 24/7 and typically arrive within 45 minutes of request confirmation.

02. Do you service both residential and commercial? ▼

Yes. Our teams are split into specialized units for Residential (home wiring, panels) and Commercial (industrial, 3-phase, high load) environments.

03. Are your technicians fully licensed and insured? ▼

Absolutely. Every master and journeyman on the ProVolt network carries state licensing, comprehensive liability insurance, and undergoes rigorous safety drills.

04. How is pricing calculated? ▼

We use a transparent flat-book algorithm. After diagnostic inspection, you receive a precise quote. No hourly inflation, no hidden fees.

05. Do you offer warranties on installations? ▼

Yes. Standard residential installations carry a minimum 2-year warranty on labor, and up to 10+ years on solar and generator systems depending on the manufacturer.
